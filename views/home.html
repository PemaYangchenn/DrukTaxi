Druk Taxi is a software development project aimed at addressing the challenges faced by the taxi industry in recent times. With the introduction of city bus systems, the flaws of the industry have become more apparent, resulting in a decline in its use.

Register as our driver today!

Through this platform, passengers will have access to a wider network of taxi drivers, reducing the time and cost involved in finding a suitable ride. The project seeks to make the taxi industry more competitive in the face of increasing competition from other transportation services.
